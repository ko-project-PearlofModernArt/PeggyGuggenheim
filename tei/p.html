Art of This Century
May 5, 1947
Miss Betty Parson

15 East 57th Street

New York City

30 WEST FIFTY SEVENTH STREET
Dear Betty;

This is to confirm our telephoneconversation
aboutJackson Pollock.I am very pleased that you are going to
take him on in your gallery and give him a show next winter.
From now on he will bring his work to you and you will sell it
taking the usual one third discount and remitting the other
two thirds of the sales to me. Before leaving I will send you
some of his unsold paintings of the past few years and I will
give you all the data necessary concerning him whenever you
like. Please let me know when I can send the paintings to you.
Either late May or the first days of June.

Yours sincerely,
Peggy Guggenheim
